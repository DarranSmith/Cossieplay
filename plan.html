Mission

The Cozzieplay web site is intended to increase revenue for Cossieplay by promoting monthly cosplay parties and costume making services offered by the company. This is through means of generating new and repeat customers.

Success Evaluation

As this is the first website used by Cossieplay, all other advertising will be consistent for a 3 month period so that any increase in sales and attendance numbers will be attributed to the website. The figures will be compared to that of the previous 3 months and then to the same months of previous years to account for annual fluctuations. 

Target Audience

As the current clientele is predominantly older (35-50) the target site will be targeted at a younger audience of high school and young adults, both new and old to cosplay.
