Party Guidelines

While Cossieplay adhere to all safety guidelines, CossiePlay party patrons are ultimately responsible for their own safety and the safety of others. For health reasons please check with your doctor before attending, and always enjoy yourself at Cossieplay events.

Event fees must be paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS accepted. Credit card payments attract a 2% surcharge.

Alcohol is served at the bar to over 18s only. No intoxicated persons will be served. At the discression of Cossieplay staff intoxicated patrons will be escorted to a taxi at their own expense.
