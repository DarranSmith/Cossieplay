Custom designs

We custom make outstanding individually tailored costumes directly for each specific customer. Our costumes range from simple designs to advanced features like armour, animatronics, realistic weaponry and working force fields.

Due to the high quality and the range of difficulty, construction times may vary from between 1 and 50 weeks.

Prices vary from between $10 and $10,000, contact us for more information.
