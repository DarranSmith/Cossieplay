Who is Cossieplay

CossiePlay is a family-run business, operating in Viennaville since 2011. We create very high quality, individual, custom-made costumes. Anything from a simple Spiderman or Elf costumes through to costumes that include advanced features like animatronics, armour, realistic weaponry and working force fields. We buy and sell second-hand costumes a current price list is available as well.

What we do

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the Viennaville town hall. Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17 and kids under 6 are free. Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick. Parties start at 6pm and run until 11pm. There is always a live DJ at the party who takes requests and dancing is highly encouraged (but not required). Signup below or on the sign-up page.
